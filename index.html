PRINCIPAL'S MESSAGE

Established in the year 1983, Sant Guru Ghasidas Govt. P.G. College, Kurud is continuously moving ahead on the path of success and is counted as one of the leading colleges of Chhattisgarh. It's academic excellency is reflected in its learning, teaching process in Arts, Commerce, Science and computer science faculties. Here post graduate classes are conducted in 13 subjects. Self finance employment oriented programs such as PGDCA are conducted which are highly beneficial for youth. Honorable Mrs. Tarini Chandrakar is an active President of college Janbhagidari committee, whose enthusiastic affords and consistent cooperation is leading the college towards excellence.

Dear Students,

We welcome you in this new session. We assure you that you will definitely enjoy and will get impressed and influenced by the academic and cultural environment of our institution.

I WISH A BRIGHT FUTURE TO ALL OF YOU.
Dr. O. P. Chandrakar
Principal
